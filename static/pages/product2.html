Sports Arena

The Aspire club sports infrastructure is best suited for corporate events and parties where we offer customized package to encourage team spirit and sport culture. Club is spread across two acre of area and offer fun and enjoyment in a safe and green environment. We are developing grounds for out door activity like Cricket, Football, Tennis, Basketball. In future club will also develop gymnasium, swimming pool, indoor activities like Badminton, Table Tennis. These facilities will be available for members and non-members for various sports activities.
